III. Compliance & SEO Strategy

Our strategy integrates SEO with BCI requirements, turning regulation into a competitive advantage.

A. Compliance-First Content Protocol

●	Non-Solicitation: All CTAs will be soft and informational (e.g., "Review Our Practice Brief" or "Request Informational Consultation"), strictly avoiding direct solicitation or aggressive selling.2
●	Mandatory Disclaimers: The site will feature a prominent, required checkbox on all forms and a dedicated page stating that content is for general education only and does not establish an attorney-client relationship or privilege.11
●	Attorney Authority: All content will be attributed to specific attorneys, linking to their detailed profile pages to boost E-E-A-T scores.14

B. SEO Technical Implementation
●	Structured Data: Comprehensive implementation of JSON-LD Schema (LawFirm, LocalBusiness, FAQPage, Article) via Next SEO for rich snippets and enhanced search understanding.15
●	Hub-and-Spoke Model: The 17 services and 8 markets are architected into vertical content pillars, maximizing internal link equity and topical authority across all specialty areas.6
●	Local SEO: Meticulous consistency of NAP (Name, Address, Phone) data across the website and Google Business Profile to dominate location-specific searches ("lawyer near me in [City]").16
